Frequently Asked Questions

1. What exactly does a media consultant do?

I act as your guide and partner in getting your story out there. This means I help you figure out the most compelling parts of your work, plan how to share it (like through press releases or social media), connect you with the right journalists, and handle all the details of things like press conferences. My goal is to make sure your message is clear, professional, and reaches the people who need to hear it.

2. How do you measure the success of a media campaign?

Success looks different for every project, but we define it together from the start. It could be counting the number of news articles published, tracking a increase in website visitors, measuring growth in your social media followers, or seeing more people attend your events. I provide you with regular reports so you can see the tangible results of our work.

3. What do I need to prepare for a press conference or major media interview?

Don't worry, I'll guide you through every step. We'll work together to create key messages, anticipate questions, and prepare talking points. I can also help with logistical details like the venue, invitations to journalists, and creating media kits. My job is to make sure you feel confident, prepared, and focused on delivering your message.

4. How is working with you different from hiring a social media manager?

While I do manage social media, I offer a bigger picture. A social media manager typically focuses on daily posts and engagement. I create an overall strategy that often combines social media with earned media (getting you featured in news outlets), event promotion, and storytelling. This integrated approach ensures all your communications work together to build a stronger, more credible reputation.

5. What's the first step to working together?

It starts with a conversation. We'll schedule a call to discuss your goals, your project, and what you hope to achieve. From there, I'll create a custom plan and quote for you. There's no obligation, and it gives you a clear idea of how we can amplify your impact.
